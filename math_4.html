Question 5.

Mathematics - 2022

(a) Express \(x^2 + 8x + 15\) in the form \((x + a)^2 + b\).

(b) Hence, or otherwise, state the coordinates of the turning point of the graph of \(f(x) = x^2 + 8x + 15\).

Marks: 4 Topic: algebra

Tips

  • For part (a), complete the square to express the quadratic in the required form.
  • For part (b), remember that the turning point of a quadratic in the form \((x + a)^2 + b\) is at \((-a, b)\).
