Question 1.

Applications of Mathematics - 2022

A survey was carried out to find the number of hours of television watched per week by a group of students. The results are shown in the table below.

Hours of TV per week Number of students
0-5 8
6-10 12
11-15 15
16-20 10
21-25 5

Calculate an estimate of the mean number of hours of television watched per week.

Marks: 3 Topic: statistics
